Welcome to this space. I plan to share insights, experiments, and lessons learned from working at the intersection of machine learning research and production systems.

## What to Expect

This blog will focus on:

- **Applied ML engineering:** Shipping reliable systems that respect latency, cost, and safety constraints.
- **Topic modeling + graph learning:** Structure-aware methods for text, signals, and multi-modal data.
- **RAG and knowledge systems:** Building retrieval pipelines that remain trustworthy under drift.
- **Bayesian decision-making:** Communicating uncertainty and making better choices under constraints.

## The Philosophy

I believe the best ML systems emerge from tight collaboration between researchers who push theoretical boundaries and engineers who understand production constraints. Neither perspective alone is sufficient—we need models that are both principled and practical.

Expect concise write-ups, reproducible code snippets, and reflections on how to move research into production without sacrificing rigor.
